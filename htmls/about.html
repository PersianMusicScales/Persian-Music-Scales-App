Persian Music Scales

Persian Music Digital Projects

Interactive tools for hearing and understanding musical systems

I created Persian Music Scales to make the interval structures, microtones, tonal relationships, and transpositions of Persian music easier to explore visually and aurally.

The project combines circular scale visualization, listening tools, a tuner, and local audio analysis. It is intended for musicians, students, teachers, researchers, and anyone interested in Persian music.

Le projet réunit une visualisation circulaire, des outils d’écoute, un accordeur et une analyse audio locale.
