Persian Music Scales

About

I created this app to share my passion for Persian music and make its rich traditions more accessible to everyone. It features interactive tools and resources to help you explore the scales, their structure, and their cultural significance.

Whether you're a musician, a student, or simply curious about Persian music, this app is here to guide you on an inspiring journey through this beautiful art form.
